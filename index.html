Elden Ring

Type: Video Game

Elden Ring is currently one of my favorite games. It is in the same genre as Dark Souls series.


The reason I'm enjoying it so much is because this game has a lot of variety and I absolutely love roleplaying games and fantasy scenes. You can be using a strength or dexterity build, be a wizard or a priest with cool spells.


The game also features Player vs Player (PvP) action.

Black Clover

Type: Anime & Manga

Black Clover is among my top choices when it comes to entertainment. It is a manga series which also got an anime adaption. The world is set in the medieval era and every person has a unique magic attribute and different spells.


Our main character has not been blessed with mana, however he has developed a thing called anti-magic, which is technically the antithesis of magic. Using his power and wits he has to outdo everyone who's been blessed from the start.


His tagline is "My magic is never giving up".
